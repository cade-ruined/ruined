# Ruined content guide

The site now has a complete visual/content structure. The items below are the
remaining real-world inputs that should replace placeholders before launch.

## Home / journey

- One concise seasonal statement: 8–16 words, used before or during the walk.
- Confirm the room names and final order: Store → Work → About → Events.
- A single current release status: available, preview, waitlist, or closed.
- One primary action per room; avoid competing links while the sequence moves.
- Final legal name, copyright line, coordinates, and public contact address.

## Store

- Front, back, detail, and on-body image for every SKU (minimum four images).
- Product name, code, price, colour, size run, inventory state, and release end.
- Measurements/fit notes, materials, origin, care, shipping weight, and lead time.
- Edition quantity or production method when scarcity is part of the story.
- Shopify handles, variants, and alternate image data once the placeholder drop
  is replaced.

## Work archive

- One landscape cover plus 4–10 supporting images or clips per project.
- One-sentence thesis, longer project narrative, discipline, client, year, and
  completion status.
- Credits for collaborators, photography, fabrication, and commissioning body.
- Location and scope where relevant; awards/press only when meaningful.
- A deliberate featured-project order rather than relying on publication date.

## About

- Final studio manifesto (75–150 words) and a one-line positioning statement.
- Current studio portrait or workspace images with photographer credits.
- Founder/team names and roles, or an explicit decision to remain anonymous.
- Exact commission categories, typical project scale, availability, and region.
- Press biography, downloadable credentials PDF, and social links if required.

## Events

- Confirmed title, date, start/end time, venue, address, capacity, and access.
- Ticket/RSVP destination plus clear free, paid, waitlist, or sold-out status.
- Event hero image, organiser/participant credits, and a 30–60 word description.
- Cancellation, accessibility, age, photography, and arrival guidance.
- Archive status after the event, ideally with 6–12 documentation images.

## Image delivery

- Product: portrait 4:5, minimum 1600 × 2000 px.
- Work covers: landscape 16:10, minimum 2000 px wide.
- Event cards: landscape 16:10; event hero may also use 4:5 portrait.
- About/studio: a mix of 3:2 landscape and 4:5 portrait.
- Export clean masters; let Next.js generate delivery sizes and formats.
