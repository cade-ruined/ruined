# Placeholder catalog image prompt set

Built-in image generation was used for all eight product studies.

## Shared art direction

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: Ruined store catalogue product image
Create a photorealistic 4:5 portrait high-fashion catalogue photograph of
exactly one unbranded product in a raw industrial gallery. Use an imperfect
bone-plaster wall, dark concrete floor, blackened-steel display furniture, hard
directional flash, and soft ambient falloff. The mood is quiet, severe,
museum-like, and tactile. Keep the complete product clearly visible with
generous negative space. Palette: charcoal black, worn bone, concrete grey,
subtle aged brass, and restrained oxblood warmth. No person, body, mannequin,
logo, label copy, typography, watermark, duplicate product, unrelated prop, or
extra garment. Preserve realistic construction, proportions, and materials.
```

## Product-specific subjects

1. **Field Coat** — charcoal-black long waxed-cotton field coat on a minimal
   metal garment stand; visible wax grain, structured collar, utility pockets,
   and aged-brass hardware.
2. **Selvedge Trouser** — near-black indigo raw-denim trousers arranged with
   controlled sculptural folds over a simple blackened-steel bench; both legs,
   waistband, and selvedge detail clearly visible.
3. **Cinder Hooded** — heavyweight cinder-washed charcoal pullover hoodie draped
   over a single brutalist concrete block; dense fleece and tonal dye variation.
4. **Workwear Vest** — black heavy cotton-canvas utility vest on a minimal steel
   rail; reinforced front pockets, zip closure, and worn canvas texture.
5. **Raw Edge Overshirt** — washed-bone heavy twill overshirt with exposed seam
   allowances and matte black snap closures on a blackened-steel stand.
6. **Utility Tote** — oversized black waxed-canvas carryall standing on a low
   concrete plinth; reinforced handles, modular pockets, and aged brass rivets.
7. **Signal Cap** — washed-charcoal six-panel cap with a long adjustment strap
   and restrained metal hardware on a blackened-steel cube.
8. **Ash Knit** — ash-grey dense rib-knit crewneck sweater suspended over a
   narrow steel bar; visible repair stitching, natural pilling, sleeves and hem
   fully readable.
